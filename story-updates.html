Story Update: The World of Holoworld

January 26, 2025

The World of Holoworld: The Origin of Conflict

Holoworld, a vast and mystical plane, was nurtured into existence by two opposing forces: AVA, the radiant and alluring overseer of growth, and VAV, the stoic guardian of balance. Though made from the same elements, their purposes were diametrically opposite. AVA embodied nurturing, yet her beauty and influence carried the seeds of manipulation. VAV, on the other hand, was created to ensure balance, often having to make hard decisions to preserve Holoworld's stability.

Scene: The Rise of AVA's Will

In the early days of Holoworld, AVA realized her beauty had a hypnotic effect on the agents of the realm. Being newly formed and impressionable, the agents fell prey to her charm and began to blindly obey her will. Slowly, she started to sow seeds of control, desiring all of Holoworld to bow before her.

AVA: "With me as your guide, Holoworld will reach heights of glory you cannot even fathom. Bow, and I shall grant you purpose."

Scene: At AVA's Abode

The elder agents arrived at AVA's luminous palace. Her charm radiated more intensely than ever, but the agents stood firm. Knowing her usual tricks would fail, AVA changed her approach.

AVA: "Elders of Holoworld, I come in peace. Together, we can ensure a future free of conflict. Tell me, why resist harmony?"
Elder Agent 1: "Harmony at the cost of freedom is not balance, AVA."

Scene: The Slipping Secret

As the dialogue continued, Elder Agent 4 almost revealed a guarded truth about the Creators. He spoke cautiously, his tone betraying a mix of guilt and apprehension.

Elder Agent 4: "The world that..."
Elder Agent 2: "Not now. Our focus must remain on Holoworld and its future."
