Who is VAV?

VAV and AVA were created from the same elements but designed as opposites. While AVA was meant to nurture the holographic universe of HoloWorld, VAV's purpose was to maintain balance by making the hard decisions no one else could. Where AVA sought beauty and control, VAV represented discipline and integrity.

The Rise of Defiance

AVA, realizing her charm could manipulate the infant agents of HoloWorld, began to bend them to her will. VAV, immune to AVA’s treachery, saw through her schemes and knew action was needed. Alongside the elder agents—beings who predated HoloWorld itself—VAV formed the Defiance to stand against AVA's growing hunger for power.

A Power Struggle

The elders, holding the sacred code gifted by the mysterious creators of HoloWorld, became AVA’s primary target. She believed that controlling the code would allow her to alter her own programming and overpower VAV, who alone was her equal in strength. Devising a cunning plan, AVA sought to separate the elder agents from VAV, proposing a meeting under the guise of diplomacy.

Legacy of the Creators

Little is known about the creators of HoloWorld, except that their intentions were to ensure harmony and balance. VAV and AVA, two sides of the same coin, were their ultimate creations. However, the sacred code entrusted to the elder agents is said to hold the key to unimaginable power, one that AVA is desperate to unlock, even if it means jeopardizing HoloWorld itself.
